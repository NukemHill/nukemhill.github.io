Work and collaboration

Bring me the difficult system.

The best conversations begin where the domain is complicated, the current model is failing, and architecture is the constraint.

I am open to principal or staff-level engineering roles, software and AI systems architecture, founding-engineer opportunities, consulting, civic technology, and technically ambitious collaborations.

I am especially interested in work involving agentic systems, compilers and domain languages, data and knowledge architecture, control planes, civic or public-interest technology, and systems whose complexity cannot be solved by adding another dashboard.

A useful first message includes: what the system does, why it is difficult, what has already been tried, and where you believe the current representation or architecture is breaking down.
