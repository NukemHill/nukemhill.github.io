Profile

Software architect and technical strategist with more than thirty years of experience spanning enterprise systems, databases, distributed applications, mobile software, consulting, product design, and technical leadership. Current work concentrates on deterministic AI execution, multi-agent coordination, compiler architecture, domain-specific languages, civic intelligence, and provenance-preserving knowledge systems.

I work best where the primary constraint is not typing code but discovering the model that makes a difficult system coherent. I move between problem framing, architecture, implementation, data design, documentation, and hands-on validation.
